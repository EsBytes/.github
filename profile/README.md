## <img src="https://devicon-website.vercel.app/api/linux/original.svg" width="24"/> Who We Are

We are **EsBytes**—a collective of passionate developers driven by curiosity and the desire to build meaningful technology. We thrive on tackling complex, challenging software engineering problems, turning intricate logic into robust solutions that leave the world a little better than we found it.

---

## 🚀 Our Focus

*   **Impactful Ecosystems:** Building software designed to solve real-world problems, enhance workflows, and scale effectively.
*   **Challenging Architecture:** Diving deep into systems engineering, performance optimization, and modern design patterns.
*   **Dual-Model Approach:** We engineer both **open-source projects** to give back to the global developer community, alongside proprietary **closed-source solutions** tailored for specific use cases.

---

## ⚡ Fun Fact

Our journey began together within the halls of an educational institute, where we bonded over late-night debugging sessions and ambitious side projects. Today, we are executing a vision that extends far beyond the classroom building software that makes a tangible difference out in the wild.

---

## 🤝 Contribution Guidelines

Because we manage a diverse portfolio of open-source and proprietary software, our contribution model varies by project:

*   **Public Community Projects:** Contributions are highly welcome on repos explicitly marked with a contribution guide (`CONTRIBUTING.md`). Feel free to open issues or pull requests on these!
*   **Internal & Proprietary Projects:** Some repositories are maintained strictly by our core team or kept closed-source. We do not accept external contributions on these projects.
